OUR PROGRAM

We focus on solving 3 critical issues plaguing the startup ecosystem: people-fitness, business-model fitness, and scalability-fitness.

Most incubations focus on fundraising, and not enough on upskilling the team; creating a lack of people-fitness.

Most founders are technology experts, but what they lack is business development, and customer acquisition expertise; which contributes to the lack of business-model fitness.

Most startups fail to create hypergrowth after promising such growth during angel, pre-seed or seed-level investments.

Our programs are crafted, our mentors are curated and our startups are hand-picked keeping those gaps in mind.
